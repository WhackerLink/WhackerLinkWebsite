About Us

WhackerLink FiveM is an advanced, feature-rich, free, and open-source Radio P25 script designed specifically for the FiveM platform, enhancing the realism and immersion of role-playing servers. With a focus on authenticity, reliability, and user-friendly functionality, WhackerLink FiveM aims to provide a seamless communication experience for all users.
